
Books
=====

Free / Open Access
------------------

- [Market Segmentation Analysis](https://www.amazon.com.br/Market-Segmentation-Analysis-Understanding-Professionals-ebook/dp/B07FQDSF3X/ref=as_li_ss_tl?__mk_pt_BR=%C3%85M%C3%85%C5%BD%C3%95%C3%91&keywords=data+science&qid=1585076576&refinements=p_36:5560478011&rnid=5560477011&s=digital-text&sr=1-27&swrs=C62D3DF592FFF3147FC9C281853AEE94&linkCode=sl1&tag=rafaeljsm-20&linkId=6c80c1e4a0f03afc9f5ff74699696766&language=pt_BR) — Classic reference on cluster-based market segmentation, free via Amazon.

- [Dive into Deep Learning](https://d2l.ai/) — Aston Zhang, Zachary Lipton, Mu Li & Alexander Smola. Interactive, runnable textbook covering modern deep learning with PyTorch, JAX, and TensorFlow. Adopted by 500+ universities worldwide. Published by Cambridge University Press in 2023; fully free online.

- [Probabilistic Machine Learning: An Introduction](https://probml.github.io/pml-book/book1.html) — Kevin P. Murphy (MIT Press, 2022). Rigorous, mathematically thorough treatment of ML from a probabilistic perspective. The full PDF is freely downloadable from the official GitHub. A companion volume, *Advanced Topics* (2023), covers diffusion models and generative modeling.

- [Python for Data Analysis, 3rd Edition](https://wesmckinney.com/book/) — Wes McKinney (O'Reilly, 2022). The definitive guide to pandas, NumPy, and Jupyter for data wrangling. O'Reilly allows the author to host the full text free on his website.

- [Speech and Language Processing, 3rd Edition (draft)](https://web.stanford.edu/~jurafsky/slp3/) — Daniel Jurafsky & James H. Martin (Stanford, updated 2024). The canonical NLP textbook, now fully updated to cover transformers, LLMs, and modern ASR. Free draft PDF available.

Paid — LLMs & Generative AI
----------------------------

- [Hands-On Large Language Models](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/hands-on-large-language/9781098150952/) — Jay Alammar & Maarten Grootendorst (O'Reilly, 2024). Richly illustrated, practical introduction covering embeddings, RAG, fine-tuning, and transformer internals. One of O'Reilly's best-selling AI titles of 2024.

- [Build a Large Language Model (From Scratch)](https://www.manning.com/books/build-a-large-language-model-from-scratch) — Sebastian Raschka (Manning, 2024). Step-by-step guide to implementing a GPT-like LLM in PyTorch from the ground up, from tokenisation to RLHF. Companion code on [GitHub](https://github.com/rasbt/LLMs-from-scratch).

- [AI Engineering: Building Applications with Foundation Models](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/ai-engineering/9781098166298/) — Chip Huyen (O'Reilly, 2025). Covers the full lifecycle of building production AI products with LLMs and multimodal models — evaluation, RAG, agents, fine-tuning, and cost management. The most-read book on O'Reilly's platform since its release.

- [Natural Language Processing with Transformers, Revised Edition](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/natural-language-processing/9781098136789/) — Lewis Tunstall, Leandro von Werra & Thomas Wolf (O'Reilly, 2022). Written by core Hugging Face team members; covers fine-tuning, cross-lingual transfer, distillation, and efficient deployment.

- [Generative Deep Learning, 2nd Edition](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/generative-deep-learning/9781098134174/) — David Foster (O'Reilly, 2023). Hands-on coverage of VAEs, GANs, diffusion models, and transformers for generating images, text, audio, and more.

Paid — MLOps & Production ML
-----------------------------

- [Designing Machine Learning Systems](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/designing-machine-learning/9781098107956/) — Chip Huyen (O'Reilly, 2022). Holistic framework for building reliable, scalable, and maintainable ML systems — from data pipelines and feature engineering to monitoring and retraining strategies.

- [Fundamentals of Data Engineering](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/fundamentals-of-data/9781098108298/) — Joe Reis & Matt Housley (O'Reilly, 2022). End-to-end coverage of the data engineering lifecycle: ingestion, storage, transformation, orchestration, and serving. An instant classic in the field.

Paid — Classic Must-Reads
--------------------------

- [Python Feature Engineering Cookbook](https://www.packtpub.com/product/python-feature-engineering-cookbook/9781789806311) — Soledad Galli (Packt). Practical recipes for building and transforming features for machine learning, using scikit-learn and other Python tools.

- [Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow, 3rd Edition](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/hands-on-machine-learning/9781098125967/) — Aurélien Géron (O'Reilly, 2022). The go-to practical ML book for beginners and intermediate practitioners. Covers the full scikit-learn and Keras/TensorFlow ecosystem with end-to-end projects.
