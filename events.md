Events
======

## Industry & Practitioner Conferences

- [AWS Innovate (Generative AI & Data)](https://aws.amazon.com/events/aws-innovate/) - Free virtual series from AWS covering generative AI and data, runs across multiple regions (Americas, EMEA, APJ)
- [Big Data London (Big Data LDN)](https://bigdataldn.com/) - UK's leading data, analytics and AI conference & exhibition; annual, September, London Olympia; free to attend
- [Coalesce (dbt Labs)](https://coalesce.getdbt.com/) - Premier analytics engineering conference; annual, October, Las Vegas
- [Congresso Nacional de Ciência de Dados (CONACD)](https://conacd.com.br/) - Brazil's largest data science congress, organized by ABRACD; annual, December
- [DataBricks Data+AI Summit](https://databricks.com/dataaisummit) - Annual data and AI conference covering data engineering, ML, and LLMs; June, San Francisco + virtual
- [Gartner Data & Analytics Summit](https://www.gartner.com/en/conferences/calendar/data-analytics) - Annual summits for CDOs and data/analytics leaders; multiple locations (US, UK, APAC)
- [NLP Summit / Applied AI Summit (John Snow Labs)](https://www.nlpsummit.org/) - Free virtual event; Healthcare NLP Summit (April) and Applied AI Summit (October); formerly NLP Summit, expanded scope in 2025
- [NVIDIA GTC](https://www.nvidia.com/gtc/) - GPU Technology Conference; NVIDIA's flagship AI developer conference; annual, March, San Jose + Washington D.C.
- [ODSC (Open Data Science Conference)](https://odsc.com/) - Global practitioner-focused conferences covering ML, LLMs, MLOps, and generative AI; East (Boston, May), West (San Francisco, autumn), Europe (London)
- [TDWI (Transforming Data With Intelligence)](https://tdwi.org/) - Data and analytics education conferences; multiple events per year across US cities (TDWI Transform series)
- [World Summit AI](https://worldsummit.ai/) - Global AI summit; annual, October, Amsterdam; covers enterprise AI, generative AI, LLMs, and AI governance

## New & Emerging Conferences (2022–2025)

- [AI Engineer World's Fair](https://www.ai.engineer/) - Technical AI engineering conference; annual, June, San Francisco; covers LLMs, agents, AI infrastructure; launched 2023
- [Google Cloud Next](https://cloud.withgoogle.com/next) - Google's annual cloud + AI conference; April, Las Vegas; strong generative AI and data analytics track
- [The AI Conference](https://aiconference.com/) - Applied AI conference founded 2023 by former O'Reilly AI Conference program chair; annual, September, San Francisco

## Academic & Research Conferences

- [ACL (Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics)](https://aclweb.org/) - Premier NLP/computational linguistics conference; annual, July–August
- [CVPR (IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition)](https://cvpr.thecvf.com/) - Premier computer vision conference; annual, June; Nashville in 2025
- [EMNLP (Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing)](https://aclanthology.org/venues/emnlp/) - Top NLP research conference; annual, November
- [ICLR (International Conference on Learning Representations)](https://iclr.cc/) - Premier deep learning research conference; annual, April–May
- [ICML (International Conference on Machine Learning)](https://icml.cc/) - Flagship machine learning research conference; annual, July; Vancouver in 2025
- [KDD (ACM SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining)](https://kdd.org/) - Premier data mining and knowledge discovery conference; annual, August
- [MLSys (Conference on Machine Learning and Systems)](https://mlsys.org/) - Research at the intersection of ML and systems; annual, May
- [NeurIPS (Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems)](https://neurips.cc/) - One of the most prestigious ML/AI research conferences; annual, December

## Defunct / Discontinued

- **O'Reilly Artificial Intelligence Conference** - DEFUNCT (ended 2020). O'Reilly cancelled all in-person conferences. Partial successor: [The AI Conference](https://aiconference.com/) (independent, launched 2023, run by former O'Reilly AI Conference program chair) and [O'Reilly AI Codecon](https://www.oreilly.com/conferences/) (virtual series launched 2025).
- **O'Reilly Strata Data & AI Conference** - DEFUNCT (ended 2021). O'Reilly pivoted to online learning and virtual Superstream series. No direct replacement; Databricks Data+AI Summit and Coalesce (dbt Labs) fill similar practitioner space.
