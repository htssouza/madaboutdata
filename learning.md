Courses
------

### Foundational

- [Coursera Data Science Specialization - Johns Hopkins University](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/jhu-data-science) - Paid (audit free)
- [Machine Learning Specialization - DeepLearning.AI / Stanford (Andrew Ng, 2022 rebuild)](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/machine-learning-introduction) - Paid (audit free)
- [Deep Learning Specialization - DeepLearning.AI (Andrew Ng)](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/deep-learning) - Paid (audit free)
- [Stanford CS229: Machine Learning](https://cs229.stanford.edu/) - Free (lecture notes & materials)
- [NYU Deep Learning - Yann LeCun / Alfredo Canziani (2021)](https://atcold.github.io/NYU-DLSP21/) - Free

### Computer Vision & NLP

- [Stanford CS231N: Deep Learning for Computer Vision](https://cs231n.stanford.edu/) - Free (slides & notes)
- [Stanford CS224N: NLP with Deep Learning](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs224n/) - Free (slides, notes & YouTube)
- [Stanford - Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare](https://online.stanford.edu/programs/artificial-intelligence-healthcare) - Paid

### Practical Deep Learning

- [Practical Deep Learning for Coders - Part 1 (fast.ai)](https://course.fast.ai/) - Free
- [Practical Deep Learning for Coders - Part 2: Stable Diffusion & Transformers from scratch (fast.ai)](https://course.fast.ai/Lessons/part2.html) - Free

### LLMs & Generative AI

- [Generative AI with Large Language Models - DeepLearning.AI / AWS](https://www.coursera.org/learn/generative-ai-with-llms) - Paid (audit free)
- [Generative AI for Everyone - DeepLearning.AI (Andrew Ng)](https://www.deeplearning.ai/courses/generative-ai-for-everyone/) - Free
- [LLM Course - Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/learn/llm-course/chapter1/1) - Free
- [Short Courses: LLMOps, RAG, Agents, Fine-tuning - DeepLearning.AI](https://www.deeplearning.ai/courses/) - Free (most)
- [Generative AI for Beginners - Microsoft (21 lessons)](https://github.com/microsoft/generative-ai-for-beginners) - Free
- [AI Agents for Beginners - Microsoft (12 lessons)](https://github.com/microsoft/ai-agents-for-beginners) - Free
